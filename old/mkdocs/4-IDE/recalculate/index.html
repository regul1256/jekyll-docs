History recalculation#

Briefly about the history recalculation mechanism.

The history of Virtual Tags and Accumulators can be recalculated. This is due to their dependency on a source tag and/or an expression that define values for both tag types.

Click the Recalculate History icon to open the menu. You can choose one of three types of source data in the same-named menu item:

  1. Raw
    Recalculate un-aggregated history data.
  2. Hours
    Recalculate history data that is aggregated by hours.
  3. Days
    Recalculate history data that is aggregated by days.

The Whole history period flag is active by default. You can also set the period of recalculation in the Time controls menu below the flag.
